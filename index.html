Cross-species Consensus Atlas of the Primate Basal Ganglia
Nelson J. Johansen, Yuanyuan Fu, ..., Inkar Kapen, ..., Trygve E. Bakken.
arXiv, 2025
arXiv

A multiomic consensus atlas of 1.8 million nuclei from human, macaque, and marmoset spanning eight BG structures. Integrating cross-species gene expression, open chromatin, and spatial profiling to enable the definition of conserved and divergent cell types.
